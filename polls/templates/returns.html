RETURN POLICY



 

ALL ITEMS BOUGHT FROM THE CLEARANCE SALE SECTION ARE NOT ELIGIBLE FOR RETURN/ EXCHANGE/ REFUNDS! 

Easy Returns

Our return policy lasts 10 days from the date of purchase, No Questions Asked :-)


To complete your return/exchange, Please raise a return request on our portal along with the receipt or proof of purchase. After receiving the same, we will validate & schedule a reverse pickup from your shipping address & our courier pickup partner will contact you for picking the product back.

Exchange (if applicable)

If 10 days have gone by, though, we will not be able to offer you a return/refund but we still can exchange your product till 20 days from the date of purchase.


Eligible for Return

  • Your item must be unused & in the same condition in which you received it.
  • It should be in its original packaging accompanied by a proof of purchase.
  • Any item not in its original condition is damaged or missing parts for reasons not due to our error will not be exchanged

Refunds (If Applicable)

Once your return is received, we will inspect the same.
If its approved, then your refund will be processed asap, and a credit amounting to your invoice amount, wil automatically be initiated to your original method payment. A notification will be sent to you confirming the same.

In case of rejection, We will notify you via your registered email of mobile no. You can contact us back for any further communication.

Late or missing refunds (if applicable)

* It depends on the banks or cards, the time they take for processing the refund once after getting confirmation from our end.

However, in case of non-receipt of the same post 15 days after the approval, Please follow the following steps:

  • Check your bank account again
  • Contact your bank or Credit Card issuing authority, it may take some time before your refund is officially posted

If you have done all of this and you still have not received your refund yet, please contact us at dreamwalk.info@gmail.com

Warranty

We really care about our Customers & their product experience, therefore we offer a 3 months warranty from the date of purchase.

There are be 2 types of Warranty:

  • Manufacturing Warranty: this will be applicable in case of any manufacturing defects like stitching, pasting,material defect etc.
  • Experience Warranty: this will be applicable in case your experience with the product usage is not so good after using it for a certain time within 3 months.

In both these cases, we will offer an exchange of the same product or any other product that you choose from our catalog having the same invoice value as your original invoice.

You will have to follow the exchange process as mentioned above, however, in this case the reverse & forward shipping costs will be borne by you.

Depending on where you live, the time it may take for your exchanged product to reach you may vary.

Cancellations and Modifications

You can cancel your orders before its shipped by clicking on CANCEL ITEM or contacting us, the full invoice amount will be refunded to your original payment method. Once the item is shipped it cannot be cancelled.

Can I modify the shipping address of my order after it has been placed?

Yes, You can modify the shipping address of your order before we have processed (packed) it, by updating it under 'change address' option which is available under My order' section or by contacting us.
